Pepsi is a carbonated soft drink manufactured by PepsiCo.
Originally created and developed in 1893 by Caleb Bradham
and introduced as Brad's Drink, it was renamed as Pepsi-Cola in 1898, and then shortened to P epsi in 1961.
The original recipe also included sugar and vanilla.
Bradham sought to create a fountain drink that was appealing and would aid in digestion and boost energy.
